# Lesson Plan

**Class:** Grade 3

**Subject:** Social Studies

**Duration:** 40 minutes

**Gender:** Mixed

**Topic:** Responsibilities and Social Justice I

**Subtopics:**
- Meaning of responsibility
- Personal responsibilities
- Responsibilities to family, school and community
- Being accountable
- Fulfilling duties

**Behavioral Objectives:**
By the end of this lesson, pupils should be able to:
- Define responsibility in their own words
- Identify at least two personal responsibilities
- List responsibilities they have to their family, school and community
- Explain what it means to be accountable
- State ways to fulfill their duties

**Reference Materials:** Textbooks

**Previous Knowledge:** Pupils understand basic rules at home and school

**Presentation:**
- Step 1: Educator asks pupils what they do at home without being told
- Step 2: Educator explains the meaning of responsibility using simple examples
- Step 3: Educator discusses personal responsibilities and responsibilities to family, school and community
- Step 4: Educator explains being accountable - what it means to answer for our actions
- Step 5: Educator asks pupils to share how they fulfill their duties

**Instructional Materials:** Chart showing different responsibilities at home, school and community

**Evaluation:**
CLASSWORK: Write two things you should do at home because you are responsible.
