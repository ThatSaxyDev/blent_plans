# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 3
**Subject:** Social Studies
**Topic:** Responsibilities and Social Justice I

## Lesson Content
Being responsible means doing the right thing without being told. People can depend on you.

## Key Points
- Personal: keep clean, get up on time, do homework
- Family: help with chores, respect parents, share with siblings, keep room tidy
- School: listen to teacher, do classwork, keep classroom clean, be kind to classmates
- Community: keep environment clean, respect others' belongings, help neighbors, follow rules

## Assignment
Write one thing you will do to show responsibility at school this week.
