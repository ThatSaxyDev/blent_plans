# Lesson Plan

**Class:** Grade 3

**Subject:** Social Studies

**Duration:** 40 minutes

**Gender:** Mixed

**Topic:** Responsibilities and Social Justice II

**Subtopics:**
- What is Social Justice
- Fairness and Equality
- Treating Everyone with Respect
- Standing Against Discrimination
- Justice for All

**Behavioral Objectives:**
By the end of this lesson, pupils should be able to:
- Define social justice in simple terms
- Explain why fairness and equality are important
- List ways to treat everyone with respect
- State why we must stand against discrimination
- Describe what "justice for all" means

**Reference Materials:** Social Studies Textbook

**Previous Knowledge:** Pupils know about being kind and fair from previous lessons

**Presentation:**
- Step 1: Educator asks pupils to share what being fair means to them
- Step 2: Educator explains what social justice means with simple examples
- Step 3: Educator discusses fairness, equality, and respecting others
- Step 4: Educator talks about standing against discrimination and justice for all

**Instructional Materials:** Charts, pictures showing fair treatment

**Evaluation:**
CLASSWORK: Why is it important to treat everyone fairly and with respect?
