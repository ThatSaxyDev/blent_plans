# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 3
**Subject:** Social Studies
**Topic:** Responsibilities and Social Justice II

## Lesson Content
Social justice means being fair to all people. Everyone gets the same chances.

## Key Points
- Fairness: give everyone the same thing
- Equality: everyone has the same rights
- Respect: treat others how you want to be treated
- No discrimination: don't treat people badly because of how they look or speak
- Justice: no one is above the rules, everyone gets a fair chance

## Assignment
Why is it important to treat everyone fairly?
