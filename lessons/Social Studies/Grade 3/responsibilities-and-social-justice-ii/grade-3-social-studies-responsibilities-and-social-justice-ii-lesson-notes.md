# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 3

**Subject:** Social Studies

**Topic:** Responsibilities and Social Justice II

## Lesson Content

Today we will learn about social justice. Social justice means being fair and just to all people. It means everyone gets the same chances and everyone is treated the same way.

## What is Social Justice

Social justice is about making sure everyone is treated fairly. No one should be left out or treated badly. When we practice social justice, we help make the world a better place for everyone.

## Fairness and Equality

Being fair means giving everyone the same thing. Equality means everyone has the same rights. In school, being fair means everyone gets a chance to answer questions. Being equal means everyone can learn, play, and be safe.

## Treating Everyone with Respect

Respect means treating others the way you want to be treated. We show respect when we listen to others, say please and thank you, and don't make fun of anyone. Every person deserves respect, no matter who they are.

## Standing Against Discrimination

Discrimination is when someone treats another person badly because of how they look, speak, or where they come from. We must stand against discrimination by being kind to everyone and speaking up when we see someone being treated unfairly.

## Justice for All

Justice for all means everyone should be treated fairly under the law. It means no one is above the rules and everyone gets a fair chance. We can practice justice by being honest, sharing, and helping others who are not treated fairly.
