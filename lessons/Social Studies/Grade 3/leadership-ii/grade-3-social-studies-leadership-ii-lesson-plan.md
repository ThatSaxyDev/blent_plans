# Lesson Plan

**Class:** Grade 3

**Subject:** Social Studies

**Duration:** 40 minutes

**Gender:** Mixed

**Topic:** Leadership II

**Subtopics:**
- Types of leaders
- Qualities of a good leader
- Leadership in the school
- Being a good follower

**Behavioral Objectives:**
By the end of this lesson, pupils should be able to:
- Identify two types of leaders
- State two qualities of a good leader
- Explain the role of leaders in school

**Reference Materials:** Textbooks

**Previous Knowledge:** Pupils know what a leader is from their class captain or house captain

**Presentation:**
- Step 1: Educator asks pupils to name their class captain and what the captain does
- Step 2: Educator explains different types of leaders
- Step 3: Educator discusses qualities of a good leader
- Step 4: Educator talks about leadership in the school
- Step 5: Educator explains being a good follower

**Instructional Materials:** Pictures of different leaders, chart showing qualities of a good leader

**Evaluation:**
CLASSWORK: Name one quality of a good leader.
