# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 3

**Subject:** Social Studies

**Topic:** Leadership II

## Lesson Content
A leader is someone who guides or directs others. In our homes, schools, and communities, we have leaders.

## Types of Leaders
- President: leads the whole country
- Governor: leads a state
- Obi or Emir: leads traditional communities
- Class captain: leads the class
- Teacher: leads the school

## Qualities of a Good Leader
A good leader must be:
- Honest: tells the truth and does not cheat
- Fair: treats everyone equally
- Kind: cares about others
- Brave: is not afraid to do the right thing
- Smart: makes good decisions

## Leadership in the School
The class captain helps the teacher. The head prefect helps the principal. They keep order and help other pupils.

## Being a Good Follower
A good follower listens to leaders. A good follower does the right thing. Followers support their leaders.

## Assignment
Name two qualities of a good leader.
