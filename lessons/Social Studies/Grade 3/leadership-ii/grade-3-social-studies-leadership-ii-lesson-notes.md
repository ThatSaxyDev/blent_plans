# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 3
**Subject:** Social Studies
**Topic:** Leadership II

## Lesson Content
A leader guides and directs others in homes, schools, and communities.

## Key Points
- Types of leaders: President, Governor, Obi, Emir, class captain, teacher
- Good leader must be: honest, fair, kind, brave, smart
- School leaders: class captain helps teacher, head prefect helps principal
- Good follower: listens, does the right thing, supports leaders

## Assignment
Name two qualities of a good leader.
