# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 4

**Subject:** Social Studies

**Topic:** Globalization and International Relations I

## Lesson Content
The world is becoming smaller. People in different countries can now talk, trade, and share ideas easily. This is called globalization.

## What is Globalization?
Globalization means how countries and people around the world are connected. It is like one big community. When you buy something made in another country, that is globalization. When you talk to someone abroad, that is globalization too.

## Effects of Globalization on Nigeria
### Positive Effects
- We get new and better products from other countries
- Nigerian products like cocoa and oil are sold abroad
- We learn new ideas and technology
- More jobs are created through international business

### Negative Effects
- Foreign goods may be expensive
- Some local businesses close because foreign products are cheaper
- We may lose our traditional ways

## International Trade
International trade is when countries buy and sell goods with each other. Nigeria sells oil and cocoa to other countries. Nigeria buys machines, phones, and medicines from other countries.

## Nigeria's Role in Global Affairs
Nigeria is a big country in Africa. We belong to groups like ECOWAS and the United Nations. Nigeria helps keep peace in Africa and speaks for African countries in world meetings.

## Economic Cooperation
Economic cooperation means countries working together to grow their economies. Nigeria works with other countries to build roads, share technology, and trade fairly.
