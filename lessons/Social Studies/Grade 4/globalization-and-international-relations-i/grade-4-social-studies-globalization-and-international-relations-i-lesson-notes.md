# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 4
**Subject:** Social Studies
**Topic:** Globalization and International Relations I

## Lesson Content
The world is connected through trade, talk, and ideas. This is globalization.

## Key Points
- Globalization: countries and people connected worldwide
- Positive: get good products, sell Nigerian goods (oil, cocoa), learn new ideas, more jobs
- Negative: foreign goods may be expensive, local businesses may close, may lose traditions
- Trade: Nigeria sells oil and cocoa, buys machines and phones
- Nigeria in world: belongs to ECOWAS and UN, helps keep peace in Africa

## Assignment
Name two things Nigeria sells to other countries.
