# Lesson Plan

**Class:** Grade 4

**Subject:** Social Studies

**Duration:** 40 minutes

**Gender:** Mixed

**Topic:** Globalization and International Relations I

**Subtopics:**
- What is Globalization
- Effects of Globalization on Nigeria
- International Trade
- Nigeria's Role in Global Affairs
- Economic Cooperation

**Behavioral Objectives:**
By the end of this lesson, pupils should be able to:
- Define globalization
- Identify two effects of globalization on Nigeria
- Explain what international trade means
- State one way Nigeria participates in global affairs

**Reference Materials:** Textbooks

**Previous Knowledge:** Pupils know that Nigeria is a country with neighbours and that people from other countries visit Nigeria

**Presentation:**
- Step 1: Educator asks if pupils have seen things from other countries in their homes or shops
- Step 2: Educator explains the meaning of globalization in simple terms
- Step 3: Educator discusses effects of globalization on Nigeria (positive and negative)
- Step 4: Educator explains international trade and gives examples
- Step 5: Educator talks about Nigeria's role in global affairs and economic cooperation

**Instructional Materials:** Charts showing imported and exported goods, pictures of Nigeria's flag at international events

**Evaluation:**
CLASSWORK: Globalization means the world is now ---- and people can share things across countries. (True/False)
