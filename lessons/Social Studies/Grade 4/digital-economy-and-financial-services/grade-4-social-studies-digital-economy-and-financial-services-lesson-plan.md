# Lesson Plan

**Class:** Grade 4

**Subject:** Social Studies

**Duration:** 40 minutes

**Gender:** Mixed

**Topic:** Digital Economy and Financial Services

**Subtopics:**
- What is a digital economy
- Online transactions and payments
- Mobile banking
- Benefits of digital financial services
- Risks and safety in digital transactions

**Behavioral Objectives:**
By the end of this lesson, pupils should be able to:
- Explain what a digital economy means
- Name one way people pay online
- State one benefit of mobile banking

**Reference Materials:** Textbooks

**Previous Knowledge:** Pupils know what a bank is and have seen mobile phones used for payments

**Presentation:**
- Step 1: Educator asks pupils if they have seen their parents pay with phone
- Step 2: Educator explains the digital economy
- Step 3: Educator discusses online payments and mobile banking
- Step 4: Educator talks about benefits of digital financial services
- Step 5: Educator explains risks and safety tips

**Instructional Materials:** Pictures of POS machines, mobile banking apps, chart showing benefits

**Evaluation:**
CLASSWORK: What is one benefit of mobile banking?
