# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 4
**Subject:** Social Studies
**Topic:** Digital Economy and Financial Services

## Lesson Content
A digital economy uses phones and computers to buy and sell things.

## Key Points
- Buy and sell online using internet
- Pay without cash: phone, ATM card, computer
- Mobile banking: check balance, send money, pay bills, buy airtime
- Benefits: fast, easy, safe, available 24/7
- Safety: don't share PIN, don't click unknown links, tell grown-up if something is wrong

## Assignment
Name one safety rule for mobile banking.
