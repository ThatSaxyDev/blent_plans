# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 4

**Subject:** Social Studies

**Topic:** Digital Economy and Financial Services

## Lesson Content
A digital economy is an economy based on digital technology. People buy and sell things using phones and computers.

## What is a Digital Economy?
In a digital economy, people use the internet to do business. You can buy things online. You can sell things online. Money moves digitally.

## Online Transactions and Payments
You can pay for things without cash. Use your phone, ATM card, or computer. Examples: buying data, paying for goods online, transferring money.

## Mobile Banking
Mobile banking means using your phone to bank. You can:
- Check your account balance
- Send money to someone
- Pay bills
- Buy airtime

## Benefits of Digital Financial Services
- Fast: money transfers in seconds
- Easy: no need to go to the bank
- Safe: your money is tracked
- Convenient: available 24/7

## Risks and Safety in Digital Transactions
- Do not share your PIN or password
- Do not click unknown links
- Check your account regularly
- Tell a grown-up if you see something wrong
