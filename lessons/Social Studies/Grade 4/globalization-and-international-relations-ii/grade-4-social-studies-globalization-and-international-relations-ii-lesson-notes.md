# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 4
**Subject:** Social Studies
**Topic:** Globalization and International Relations II

## Lesson Content
Organizations bring countries together to work in peace and develop together.

## Key Points
- UN (United Nations): global group for peace, includes UNICEF, UNESCO, WHO
- AU (African Union): all African countries working together, formerly OAU
- ECOWAS: West African countries trading together
- Human rights: everyone deserves education, food, peace, freedom
- Global citizen: respect others, care for environment, help those in need

## Assignment
Name one thing the UN does to help children.
