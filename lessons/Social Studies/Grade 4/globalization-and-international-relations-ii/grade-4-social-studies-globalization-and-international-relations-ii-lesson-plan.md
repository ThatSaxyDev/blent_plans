# Lesson Plan

**Class:** Grade 4

**Subject:** Social Studies

**Duration:** 40 minutes

**Gender:** Mixed

**Topic:** Globalization and International Relations II

**Subtopics:**
- United Nations and its agencies
- African Union
- ECOWAS
- International human rights
- Global citizenship

**Behavioral Objectives:**
By the end of this lesson, pupils should be able to:
- Define the United Nations and identify at least two of its agencies
- State the meaning and purpose of the African Union
- Explain what ECOWAS stands for and its importance to West Africa
- List two examples of international human rights
- Explain what global citizenship means

**Reference Materials:** Textbooks

**Previous Knowledge:** Pupils have learned about their local community, Nigeria as a nation, and basic geography of Africa

**Presentation:**
- Step 1: Educator asks pupils if they know of any groups or organizations that bring people or countries together
- Step 2: Educator introduces the United Nations (UN) and explains its agencies like UNICEF and UNESCO
- Step 3: Educator explains the African Union (AU) and ECOWAS as organizations that bring African countries together
- Step 4: Educator introduces international human rights and explains what global citizenship means
- Step 5: Educator summarises by asking pupils to share one thing they learned about these international organizations

**Instructional Materials:** Wall charts showing UN flag, African Union logo, ECOWAS logo, pictures of children enjoying their rights

**Evaluation:**
CLASSWORK: Name two international organizations that help African countries work together.
