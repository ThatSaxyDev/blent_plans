# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 4
**Subject:** Social Studies
**Topic:** Advanced Financial Literacy

## Lesson Content
Saving means keeping money for later. It helps us be ready for emergencies and achieve dreams.

## Key Points
- Save: keep money safe for the future
- Invest: use money to make more money
- Ways to save: piggy bank, bank account, cooperative
- Interest: extra money banks give you for saving
- Budget: plan your money — save some, spend some
- Goals: save for school fees, bike, or emergency
