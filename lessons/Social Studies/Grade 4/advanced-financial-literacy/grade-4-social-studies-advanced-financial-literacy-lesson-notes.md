# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 4

**Subject:** Social Studies

**Topic:** Advanced Financial Literacy

## Lesson Content
Saving money means keeping some money for the future instead of spending it all. It helps us be ready for emergencies and achieve our dreams.

## Saving and Investment
Saving is keeping money safe. Investment is using money to make more money. Both help us grow our wealth.

## Types of Savings Accounts
- Piggy bank or home savings: saving at home
- Bank savings account: saving in a bank
- Cooperative society savings: saving with a group

## Interest on Savings
When you save money in a bank, the bank says thank you by giving you extra money. This extra money is called interest. It is like a reward for saving.

## Budgeting Money
A budget is a plan for your money. It helps you know how much to save and how much to spend. Write down your income and expenses.

## Financial Goals
A goal is something you want to achieve. Your financial goals might be:
- Save for school fees
- Save for a new bicycle
- Save for emergency
