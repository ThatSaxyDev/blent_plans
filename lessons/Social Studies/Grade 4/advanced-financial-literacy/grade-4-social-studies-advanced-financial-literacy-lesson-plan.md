# Lesson Plan

**Class:** Grade 4

**Subject:** Social Studies

**Duration:** 40 minutes

**Gender:** Mixed

**Topic:** Advanced Financial Literacy

**Subtopics:**
- Saving and investment
- Types of savings accounts
- Interest on savings
- Budgeting money
- Financial goals

**Behavioral Objectives:**
By the end of this lesson, pupils should be able to:
- Explain the importance of saving money
- Name two types of savings accounts
- Explain what interest on savings means

**Reference Materials:** Textbooks

**Previous Knowledge:** Pupils know what saving money means from previous lessons

**Presentation:**
- Step 1: Educator asks pupils what they do with money they save at home
- Step 2: Educator explains the meaning and importance of saving
- Step 3: Educator discusses types of savings accounts
- Step 4: Educator explains how interest works
- Step 5: Educator talks about budgeting and financial goals

**Instructional Materials:** Chart showing savings account types, examples of piggy banks and bank books

**Evaluation:**
CLASSWORK: Why is it good to save money?
