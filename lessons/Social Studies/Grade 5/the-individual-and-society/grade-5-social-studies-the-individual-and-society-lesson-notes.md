# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 5

**Subject:** Social Studies

**Topic:** The Individual and Society

## Lesson Content

Today we will learn about how individuals relate with families and communities. We are all connected and depend on each other.

## Relationship between Self and Society

We are all part of society. Society is made up of all the people around us - our neighbours, schoolmates, and citizens of our country. As individuals, we belong to families, which belong to communities, which make up our society. Our actions affect others, and others' actions affect us.

## Relationship between Family and Community

A family is a small group of people who are related and live together. A community is a group of families living in the same area. Families make up the community. The community provides schools, markets, churches, and other places that help families. In return, families work together to make the community strong and peaceful.

## How Individuals, Families, and Communities Support Each Other

Individuals, families, and communities support each other in many ways:
- Individuals help at home and in the community
- Families provide love, care, and education to their members
- Communities provide jobs, schools, and security for families
- When one family needs help, others in the community can help
- People work together to solve problems in the community
