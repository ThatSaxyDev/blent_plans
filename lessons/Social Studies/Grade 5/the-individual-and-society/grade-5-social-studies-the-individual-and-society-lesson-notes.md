# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 5
**Subject:** Social Studies
**Topic:** The Individual and Society

## Lesson Content
We are all connected. Individuals belong to families, families belong to communities, communities make up society.

## Key Points
- Self and society: our actions affect others, others affect us
- Family: small group of related people living together
- Community: group of families in the same area
- How they support each other: individuals help at home and community, families provide love and education, communities provide jobs and schools
- Help each other: when one family needs help, others in community can help

## Assignment
How do individuals, families, and communities support each other?
