# Lesson Plan

**Class:** Grade 5

**Subject:** Social Studies

**Duration:** 40 minutes

**Gender:** Mixed

**Topic:** The Individual and Society

**Subtopics:**
- Relationship between Self and Society
- Relationship between Family and Community
- How Individuals, Families, and Communities Support Each Other

**Behavioral Objectives:**
By the end of this lesson, pupils should be able to:
- Explain the relationship between self and society
- Describe how family and community are connected
- State how individuals, families, and communities support each other

**Reference Materials:** Social Studies Textbook

**Previous Knowledge:** Pupils know about family members and their immediate community

**Presentation:**
- Step 1: Educator asks pupils to describe their family and community
- Step 2: Educator explains the relationship between self and society
- Step 3: Educator discusses how family and community are connected
- Step 4: Educator explains how all three support each other

**Instructional Materials:** Charts, pictures showing family and community activities

**Evaluation:**
CLASSWORK: How do individuals, families, and communities support each other?
