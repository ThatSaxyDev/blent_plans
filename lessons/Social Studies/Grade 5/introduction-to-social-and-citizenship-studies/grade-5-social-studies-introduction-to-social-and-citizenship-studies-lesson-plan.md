# Lesson Plan

**Class:** Grade 5

**Subject:** Social Studies

**Duration:** 40 minutes

**Gender:** Mixed

**Topic:** Introduction to Social and Citizenship Studies

**Subtopics:**
- Meaning of Social and Citizenship Studies
- Scope of Social and Citizenship Studies
- Importance for National Living
- Importance for Global Living

**Behavioral Objectives:**
By the end of this lesson, pupils should be able to:
- Define Social and Citizenship Studies
- Explain the scope of Social and Citizenship Studies
- State the importance of Social and Citizenship Studies for national living
- Describe the importance of Social and Citizenship Studies for global living

**Reference Materials:** Social Studies Textbook

**Previous Knowledge:** Pupils have basic knowledge of being good citizens

**Presentation:**
- Step 1: Educator asks pupils what it means to be a good citizen
- Step 2: Educator explains the meaning and scope of Social and Citizenship Studies
- Step 3: Educator discusses the importance for national development
- Step 4: Educator explains how Social and Citizenship Studies helps us in the global world

**Instructional Materials:** Charts, pictures showing citizenship activities

**Evaluation:**
CLASSWORK: Why is Social and Citizenship Studies important for both our country and the world?
