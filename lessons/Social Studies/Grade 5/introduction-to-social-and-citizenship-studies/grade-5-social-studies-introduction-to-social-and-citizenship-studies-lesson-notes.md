# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 5
**Subject:** Social Studies
**Topic:** Introduction to Social and Citizenship Studies

## Lesson Content
Social and Citizenship Studies teaches us how to be good citizens in our country and the world.

## Key Points
- What it teaches: our rights and duties, different cultures, national values, human rights, good leadership, environment
- Important for Nigeria: responsible citizens, national unity, love and peace, obey rules, participate in development
- Important for world: respect different cultures, join global issues like climate change, become global citizens, know human rights
