# Lesson Plan

**Class:** Grade 5

**Subject:** Social Studies

**Duration:** 40 minutes

**Gender:** Mixed

**Topic:** Norms, Values and Culture

**Subtopics:**
- Definitions of norms, values, and culture
- Importance of norms and values
- Nigerian cultural diversity

**Behavioral Objectives:**
By the end of this lesson, pupils should be able to:
- Define norms, values, and culture
- Explain why norms and values are important
- Identify different cultural groups in Nigeria

**Reference Materials:** Textbooks

**Previous Knowledge:** Pupils know about their local customs and traditions

**Presentation:**
- Step 1: Educator asks pupils to name some rules they follow at home and school
- Step 2: Educator explains the meanings of norms, values, and culture
- Step 3: Educator discusses why norms and values matter
- Step 4: Educator shows how different ethnic groups in Nigeria have different cultures

**Instructional Materials:** Charts showing Nigerian ethnic groups and their traditions

**Evaluation:**
CLASSWORK: What is the difference between norms and values?
