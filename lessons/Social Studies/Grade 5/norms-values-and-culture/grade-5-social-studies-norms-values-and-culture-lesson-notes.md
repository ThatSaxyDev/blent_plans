# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 5
**Subject:** Social Studies
**Topic:** Norms, Values and Culture

## Lesson Content
Norms and values guide how we behave. Culture is the way of life of a group of people.

## Key Points
- Norms: rules society sets for behavior, like saying please and thank you
- Values: beliefs about what is good or important, like honesty and respect
- Culture: traditions, customs, and way of life of a group of people
- Why important: keep society peaceful, teach right from wrong, bring people together
- Nigeria: over 250 ethnic groups, each with unique language, food, dress, and festivals
- Diversity: Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo, and many others — we are different but one Nigeria

## Assignment
What is the difference between norms and values?
