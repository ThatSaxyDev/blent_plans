# Lesson Plan

**Class:** Grade 4

**Subject:** History

**Duration:** 40 minutes

**Gender:** Mixed

**Topic:** States Creation in Nigeria II

**Subtopics:**
- 30 states (1991)
- 36 states (1996)
- Current states and their capitals
- Memorizing states and capitals
- Local government creation

**Behavioral Objectives:**
By the end of this lesson, pupils should be able to:
- List the number of states created in 1991 and 1996
- Name the current 36 states of Nigeria and their capitals
- Explain how to memorize states and capitals
- Describe what local governments are

**Reference Materials:** Textbooks

**Previous Knowledge:** Pupils know that Nigeria started with 12 states in 1967

**Presentation:**
- Step 1: Educator reviews the 12 states from 1967 and asks pupils to recall previous lesson
- Step 2: Educator explains the creation of 30 states in 1991 and 36 states in 1996
- Step 3: Educator lists all 36 states and their capitals
- Step 4: Educator teaches tricks to memorize states and capitals
- Step 5: Educator explains local government creation

**Instructional Materials:** Map of Nigeria, chart of states and capitals

**Evaluation:**
CLASSWORK: Name five Nigerian states and their capitals.
