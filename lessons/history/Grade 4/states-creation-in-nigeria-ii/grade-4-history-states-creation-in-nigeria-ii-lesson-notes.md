# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 4
**Subject:** History
**Topic:** States Creation in Nigeria II

## Lesson Content
Nigeria started with 12 states in 1967. Over time, more states were created for better governance.

## Key Points
- 30 states (1991): more states were created for better administration
- 36 states (1996): six more states created, this is what we have today
- Key states: Lagos (Lagos), Kano (Kano), Rivers (Port Harcourt), Delta (Asaba), Oyo (Ibadan)
- Local governments: 774 LGAs, smallest government tier, handles local roads, markets, sanitation
- Tips to memorize: group by region, learn alphabetical order, use songs
