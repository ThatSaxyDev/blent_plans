# Lesson Plan

**Class:** Grade 4

**Subject:** History

**Duration:** 40 minutes

**Gender:** Mixed

**Topic:** The Structure of Nigerian Government

**Subtopics:**
- Federal system of government
- Three arms: Executive, Legislature, Judiciary
- Three tiers of government
- Separation of powers

**Behavioral Objectives:**
By the end of this lesson, pupils should be able to:
- Explain the federal system of government
- Name the three arms of government and their functions
- Explain the three tiers of government
- State why powers are separated

**Reference Materials:** Textbooks

**Previous Knowledge:** Pupils know Nigeria has a President and governors

**Presentation:**
- Step 1: Educator asks pupils who leads Nigeria and their state
- Step 2: Educator explains federal system and three tiers of government
- Step 3: Educator teaches the three arms: Executive, Legislature, Judiciary
- Step 4: Educator explains separation of powers and why it matters

**Instructional Materials:** Chart showing the three arms of government

**Evaluation:**
CLASSWORK: What are the three arms of government?
