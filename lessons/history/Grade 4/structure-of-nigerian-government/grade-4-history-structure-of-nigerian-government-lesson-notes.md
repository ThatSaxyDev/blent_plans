# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 4
**Subject:** History
**Topic:** The Structure of Nigerian Government

## Lesson Content
Nigeria has a federal system of government. Power is shared among different levels and branches.

## Key Points
- Federal system: power shared between federal, state, and local governments
- Three tiers: Federal (Abuja), State (Governors), Local (Chairmen)
- Executive: implements laws — President, Governors
- Legislature: makes laws — National Assembly, State Assembly
- Judiciary: interprets laws — Courts, Judges
- Separation of powers: each arm works independently to check the others
- This prevents abuse of power
