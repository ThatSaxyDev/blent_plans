# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 4
**Subject:** History
**Topic:** Federal Capital in Nigeria

## Lesson Content
Every country has a capital city where the government is based. Nigeria's capital has changed over time.

## Key Points
- Lagos (1914-1991): Nigeria's capital for 77 years, center of trade and government
- Why move: Lagos was crowded, no space for growth, heavy traffic, in the south
- Abuja chosen (1991): located in middle of Nigeria, fair for all regions
- Abuja is FCT: Federal Capital Territory, belongs to all Nigerians
