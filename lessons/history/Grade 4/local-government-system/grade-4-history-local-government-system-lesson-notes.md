# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 4
**Subject:** History
**Topic:** Local Government System

## Lesson Content
Local government is the closest tier of government to the people. It handles community issues.

## Key Points
- Functions: bring government closer to the people
- Chairman: head of local government, elected by the people
- Councillors: representatives from different wards
- Services: roads, markets, sanitation, water, primary education
- Grassroots development: building infrastructure in communities
- 774 local government areas in Nigeria

## Assignment
Name one service provided by local government.
