# Lesson Plan

**Class:** Grade 4

**Subject:** History

**Duration:** 40 minutes

**Gender:** Mixed

**Topic:** Local Government System

**Subtopics:**
- Functions of local government
- Local government chairman and councillors
- Services provided
- Grassroots development

**Behavioral Objectives:**
By the end of this lesson, pupils should be able to:
- Explain what local government does
- Name the main officers in local government
- List services provided by local government
- Explain why local government matters to communities

**Reference Materials:** Textbooks

**Previous Knowledge:** Pupils know there is local government in their area

**Presentation:**
- Step 1: Educator asks pupils what local government does in their area
- Step 2: Educator explains the structure — chairman and councillors
- Step 3: Educator lists services provided by local government
- Step 4: Educator discusses grassroots development and why it matters

**Instructional Materials:** Chart of local government functions

**Evaluation:**
CLASSWORK: Name one service provided by local government.
