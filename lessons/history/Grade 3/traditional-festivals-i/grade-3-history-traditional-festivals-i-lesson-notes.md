# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 3
**Subject:** History
**Topic:** Traditional Festivals I

## Lesson Content
Nigeria has many different festivals. They are part of our rich culture and traditions.

## Key Points
- New Yam Festival (Igbo): give thanks for good harvest, families share yam dishes
- Durbar Festival (North): marks end of Ramadan, horsemen in colourful clothes, Emir leads
- Purpose: remember history and traditions, give thanks, teach younger generations
- Unity: families come together, neighbours share food, forget differences

## Assignment
What would you do to celebrate a festival with your family?
