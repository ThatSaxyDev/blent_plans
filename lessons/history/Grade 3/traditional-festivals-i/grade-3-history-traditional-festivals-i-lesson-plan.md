# Lesson Plan

**Class:** Grade 3

**Subject:** History

**Duration:** 40 minutes

**Gender:** Mixed

**Topic:** Traditional Festivals I

**Subtopics:**
- New Yam Festival (Igbo)
- Durbar Festival (Northern Nigeria)
- Purpose and significance
- How festivals bring people together

**Behavioral Objectives:**
By the end of this lesson, pupils should be able to:
- Name the New Yam Festival and the people who celebrate it
- Name the Durbar Festival and where it is celebrated
- Explain why festivals are important
- Describe how festivals bring people together

**Reference Materials:** Textbooks

**Previous Knowledge:** Pupils know that Nigeria has many different cultures and traditions

**Presentation:**
- Step 1: Educator asks pupils if they have ever attended a festival or celebration
- Step 2: Educator introduces the New Yam Festival and explains its meaning
- Step 3: Educator explains the Durbar Festival and its importance
- Step 4: Educator discusses the purpose of festivals and how they unite people

**Instructional Materials:** Pictures of festival celebrations, charts

**Evaluation:**
CLASSWORK: Name one traditional Nigerian festival and the people who celebrate it.
