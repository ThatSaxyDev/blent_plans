# Lesson Plan

**Class:** Grade 3

**Subject:** History

**Duration:** 40 minutes

**Gender:** Mixed

**Topic:** Traditional Arts and Crafts

**Subtopics:**
- Pottery and sculpture
- Weaving and cloth making
- Wood carving
- Leather work

**Behavioral Objectives:**
By the end of this lesson, pupils should be able to:
- Name different traditional arts and crafts
- Explain how pottery and weaving are done
- Identify why we must preserve traditional skills

**Reference Materials:** Textbooks

**Previous Knowledge:** Pupils have seen locally made crafts in their community

**Presentation:**
- Step 1: Educator asks pupils to name things made by hand in their community
- Step 2: Educator explains pottery, sculpture, weaving, wood carving, and leather work
- Step 3: Educator shows examples of each craft and how they are done
- Step 4: Educator discusses why we must preserve these traditional skills

**Instructional Materials:** Pictures of traditional crafts

**Evaluation:**
CLASSWORK: Name one traditional craft from your community.
