# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 3
**Subject:** History
**Topic:** Traditional Arts and Crafts

## Lesson Content
Nigerians have made beautiful things by hand for generations. These are our traditional arts and crafts.

## Key Points
- Pottery: making pots, cups, and bowls from clay
- Sculpture: shaping wood, stone, or clay into figures
- Weaving: making cloth using threads on looms
- Wood carving: shaping wood into masks, doors, and furniture
- Leather work: making bags, shoes, and belts from animal skin
- Preserve skills: pass knowledge to younger generations, keep our culture alive

## Assignment
Name one traditional craft from your community.
