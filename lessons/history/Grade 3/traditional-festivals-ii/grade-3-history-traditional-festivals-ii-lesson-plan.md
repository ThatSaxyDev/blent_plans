# Lesson Plan

**Class:** Grade 3

**Subject:** History

**Duration:** 40 minutes

**Gender:** Mixed

**Topic:** Traditional Festivals II

**Subtopics:**
- Argungu Fishing Festival
- Osun-Osogbo Festival
- Eyo Festival (Lagos)
- Unity through festivals

**Behavioral Objectives:**
By the end of this lesson, pupils should be able to:
- Name the Argungu Fishing Festival and its location
- Name the Osun-Osogbo Festival and where it is celebrated
- Name the Eyo Festival and the people who celebrate it
- Explain how festivals promote unity

**Reference Materials:** Textbooks

**Previous Knowledge:** Pupils know that Nigeria has many traditional festivals

**Presentation:**
- Step 1: Educator asks pupils what they remember about the previous lesson on festivals
- Step 2: Educator introduces the Argungu Fishing Festival
- Step 3: Educator explains the Osun-Osogbo Festival and the Eyo Festival
- Step 4: Educator discusses how festivals unite different people

**Instructional Materials:** Pictures of festival celebrations, charts

**Evaluation:**
CLASSWORK: Name one festival that brings people together and state why it is important.
