# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 3
**Subject:** History
**Topic:** Traditional Festivals II

## Lesson Content
Nigeria has many famous traditional festivals. They bring joy and show our cultures.

## Key Points
- Argungu Fishing Festival (Kebbi): men catch fish with nets, biggest fish wins prize
- Osun-Osogbo Festival (Osun): religious festival for Yoruba people, wear white, dance to river
- Eyo Festival (Lagos): only men take part, wear white hats, shows Lagos history
- Unity: festivals bring people together, forget differences, teach peace and harmony

Name one festival you would like to attend.
