# Lesson Plan

**Class:** Grade 3

**Subject:** History

**Duration:** 40 minutes

**Gender:** Mixed

**Topic:** Traditional Marriage Systems

**Subtopics:**
- Marriage customs in different ethnic groups
- Traditional engagement ceremonies
- Respect for marriage and family values

**Behavioral Objectives:**
By the end of this lesson, pupils should be able to:
- Describe how marriage works in different ethnic groups
- Explain what happens during engagement ceremonies
- State why marriage and family are important

**Reference Materials:** Textbooks

**Previous Knowledge:** Pupils know that adults get married in their families

**Presentation:**
- Step 1: Educator asks pupils about marriage ceremonies they have attended
- Step 2: Educator explains marriage customs in different ethnic groups
- Step 3: Educator describes traditional engagement ceremonies
- Step 4: Educator discusses respect for marriage and family values

**Instructional Materials:** Charts showing marriage customs

**Evaluation:**
CLASSWORK: Why is it important to respect marriage?
