# Lesson Notes

**Class:** Grade 3
**Subject:** History
**Topic:** Traditional Marriage Systems

## Lesson Content
Marriage is important in every culture. Different ethnic groups in Nigeria have their own customs.

## Key Points
- Different customs: each ethnic group has its own way of marrying
- Engagement: formal agreement before marriage, families meet and agree
- Yoruba: introduction ceremony (Introduction)
- Igbo: traditional prayer and gift-giving
- Hausa: court and solemnization
- Respect: marriage is between two families, not just two people
- Family values: love, unity, and support between husbands, wives, and children
