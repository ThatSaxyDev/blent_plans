# Lesson Plan

**Class:** Grade 5

**Subject:** History

**Duration:** 40 minutes

**Gender:** Mixed

**Topic:** Democracy and Good Governance

**Subtopics:**
- Principles of democracy
- Democratic institutions
- Free and fair elections
- Accountability and transparency
- Role of citizens in democracy

**Behavioral Objectives:**
By the end of this lesson, pupils should be able to:
- Define democracy and identify its basic principles
- Mention at least two democratic institutions in Nigeria
- Explain what free and fair elections mean
- State the importance of accountability and transparency in government
- Describe the role of citizens in a democracy

**Reference Materials:** Textbooks

**Previous Knowledge:** Pupils have learned about leadership and types of leadership in previous lessons

**Presentation:**
- Step 1: Educator asks pupils to recall what they know about leaders and leadership
- Step 2: Educator explains the meaning of democracy and its core principles
- Step 3: Educator introduces democratic institutions (executive, legislature, judiciary) and their roles
- Step 4: Educator explains free and fair elections and how accountability and transparency work
- Step 5: Educator discusses the role of citizens in a democracy and how they participate

**Instructional Materials:** Charts showing democratic institutions, pictures of elections

**Evaluation:**
CLASSWORK: Mention two (2) things citizens can do to participate in democracy.
